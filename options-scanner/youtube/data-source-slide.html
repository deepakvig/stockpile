Data Source:
Yahoo Finance — free, but know the limits
Limitations
⚠️IV can be stale on thinly traded strikes
⚠️No Greeks — delta computed from Black-Scholes
⚠️Wide spreads and low volume add noise
⚠️If it looks too good on the chart, it usually is
How to use it
✅Free, no account needed — great for getting started
✅Still surfaces real patterns — use it as a starting point
✅Always verify bid-ask on your broker before acting
🔜Finding a better data source — on the roadmap
Treat the output as a starting point for research, not a trading signal on its own.
