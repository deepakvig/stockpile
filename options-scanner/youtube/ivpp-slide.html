One
IV+pp is a comparable signal across the whole chain
Same unit across every strike and expiration — the ranking is apples-to-apples.

Under ~3 pp is noise.  5+ pp is a genuine signal.
Two
IV+pp is not your return
+5 pp means richer premium than neighbors — not a 5% return.

For actual yield on capital, look at Ann%.
IV+pp = language of volatility differences  ·  Ann% = your actual return on capital
